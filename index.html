Corée du Sud,
une Ascension Fulgurante

South Korea has become a popular destination thanks to the global popularity of its culture, particularly through K-pop, K-dramas and Korean cinema. Groups like BTS and shows like Squid Game have captivated millions of fans, sparking growing interest in the country and its customs.

Korean cuisine, with dishes like bibimbap and kimchi, also attracts visitors. They come to taste these local specialties in traditional markets and restaurants, discovering a rich and diverse culinary culture.

South Korea seduces with its landscapes and historical sites. From the ancient palaces of Seoul to the mountains of Seoraksan and the beaches of Jeju Island, the country offers a variety of natural and cultural experiences that combine tradition and modernity.
